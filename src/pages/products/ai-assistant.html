Why a standalone AI Assistant?

Most people visiting a salon website aren't ready to click "Book" right away — they have questions first. "Do you do gel extensions?" "How long does a pedicure take?" "Do you take walk-ins on Sundays?" If there's no one to answer, most of them leave. A static FAQ page doesn't change that.

Most AI chatbot products force you to rebuild your website on their platform, switch booking systems, or hand off your customer data to their marketing cloud. For a small salon that's overkill.

SICUS AI Assistant is sold standalone — you keep your Wix / Squarespace / WordPress site, you add a single script tag, and the AI starts answering visitors trained on your salon's real services and prices. Same AI that powers the full Sicus Booking platform, packaged so you can drop it in without changing anything else.

What the AI actually does on your website

Not a canned chatbot that returns "Sorry, I didn't understand that" — a real conversation that ends in a booking.

Answers service questions instantly

Pricing, durations, what's included, technician specialties, walk-in availability, parking, cancellation policy. Whatever you'd normally answer by phone or email — the AI handles it in seconds.

Recommends the right service

"I want something low-maintenance for a wedding" → AI suggests gel, nail art options, and a specific technician. Turns uncertain browsers into confident bookers by asking the right follow-up questions.

Captures bookings in-chat

Name, phone, service, preferred time — collected in natural conversation, not a five-field form visitors abandon. The booking request goes straight to your inbox or booking system.

Bilingual English + Vietnamese

Detects the visitor's language automatically and responds in kind. Critical for Vietnamese-Canadian and Vietnamese-American salon clients who prefer chatting in their first language.

24/7, no staff required

Most booking decisions happen after hours — evenings and Sunday mornings. The AI works while you're closed, capturing leads you'd otherwise lose to a competitor whose site happens to answer.

Honest when it doesn't know

If a question is outside the trained scope, the AI says so, captures the visitor's contact info, and hands the thread off to you. No hallucinations, no invented prices, no made-up policies.

3 ways to embed on your website

Pick the level that matches your website builder. We recommend the right one during onboarding.

1Script Tag (One-Line Embed)

Complexity: Lowest · Setup time: Under 1 day · Works with: Wix, Squarespace, Webflow, Shopify, custom sites, any platform that allows custom HTML in the footer

Paste a single script tag into your website's footer or custom code area. The chat bubble appears in the bottom-right corner automatically. Colors, position, and greeting message are all configured from a dashboard — no code changes needed after install. This is how 80% of our customers go live.

<!-- Paste before the closing </body> tag --> <script src="https://cdn.sicusmedia.com/assistant.js" data-salon-id="your-salon-id" defer></script>

Complexity: Lowest · Setup time: Under 1 hour · Works with: WordPress (self-hosted and WordPress.com Business plan or higher)

For WordPress sites, we provide a dedicated plugin: install from the WordPress plugin directory, enter your salon ID, and the assistant is live. No code, no footer editing, no risk of breaking your theme. Updates happen automatically whenever we ship improvements to the AI.

3API / SDK (Custom Build)

Complexity: Highest · Setup time: 1-2 weeks · Works with: Custom-built websites, native mobile apps, headless CMS setups

If you want a fully custom chat UI that matches your brand pixel-perfectly — or if you want the AI inside a native iOS / Android app — we expose a REST API and a lightweight JavaScript SDK. Your developer builds the interface; we handle the AI, the training, the conversation state, and the booking handoff. Only needed if you have a specific design requirement the script-tag embed can't meet.
