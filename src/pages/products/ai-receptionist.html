Why a standalone AI Receptionist?

The average salon misses 40% of its incoming calls — clients calling while you're mid-service, after hours, during lunch, or when you're simply too slammed to pick up. Each missed call is a booking that walked away to a competitor.

Most AI receptionist products force you to replace your entire booking system to use them. That's a non-starter for the majority of salon owners — you've already trained your staff on Fresha or Vagaro, your clients know how to book there, and the last thing you want is a 6-week migration project.

SICUS AI Receptionist is sold standalone — you keep your booking system, you add the AI on the phone, and the bookings flow into your existing calendar. Same AI that powers the full Sicus Booking platform, packaged so you can drop it in without changing anything else.

What the AI actually does on a call

Not a robot reading from a script — a conversation that feels natural and gets the booking.

Answers every call, 24/7

Zero missed calls. Calls are picked up instantly whether you're with a client, closed for the day, or slammed on a Saturday. Clients never get voicemail.

Books appointments end-to-end

Captures the client's name, service, preferred time, technician (if requested), and phone number — then confirms the booking and hands the details to your existing booking system.

Upsells automatically

Mid-week slow? The AI suggests your Tuesday happy hour. New lash service launching? It mentions it to relevant clients. Every upsell is configured by you during onboarding.

Answers FAQs on its own

Service pricing, hours, parking, cancellation policy, which techs do which services, walk-in availability. You train it once during onboarding; it handles these questions forever.

Honest when it doesn't know

If a question falls outside the trained scope, the AI captures the client's details and sends you an SMS or email to follow up — instead of inventing an answer. You stay in control of edge cases.

What a call sounds like

Real calls — tap any one to listen. The AI adapts to whatever the client actually says, not a fixed script.

3 ways to integrate with your current setup

Pick the level that matches your existing system and how much automation you want. We recommend the right one during onboarding.

1Forward + Email/SMS Handoff

Complexity: Lowest · Setup time: 1-2 days · Works with: Any booking system (even paper calendars)

You forward your salon's phone line to a dedicated SICUS number (or set it as the "busy/no-answer" forward). The AI takes the call, captures the booking details, and sends you an email or SMS with the client's name, service, time, and phone number. You (or a front desk person) manually add the booking to your existing system — same way you'd handle a phone call that you took yourself. Zero technical setup. Works with literally any booking platform or paper calendar.

2Shared Calendar (Vagaro only, today)

Complexity: Medium · Setup time: 3-5 days · Works with: Vagaro (via Google Calendar)

The AI pushes confirmed bookings into a Google Calendar that Vagaro reads from. Your team opens the imported event in Vagaro and converts it to a real appointment with one tap — faster than re-typing the details from an email. Google Calendar only, not Microsoft 365 or iCloud. Fresha, Booksy, and Square don't support this workflow — for those platforms, use Option 1 or Option 3.

3Direct API Integration

Complexity: Highest · Setup time: 1-2 weeks · Works with: Square Appointments and other platforms with open public APIs

The AI connects directly to your booking platform's API to create, modify, and cancel appointments without a middle step. When a client calls and books, the appointment appears in your salon's booking system in real time. This is the most seamless option but requires a platform with a public API — not all booking systems offer this. We confirm availability during the discovery call.
