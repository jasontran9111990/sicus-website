Who this is for

Custom salon software isn't for every salon — most independent owners should use Sicus Booking or White-Label Salon App instead. Custom builds make sense when:

  • You're a salon chain or franchise operator and need multi-location dashboards, cross-location reporting, or royalty tracking that off-the-shelf platforms don't handle well.
  • You're a beauty brand launching a salon arm and need the booking platform to match your existing brand stack and data flows.
  • You've outgrown Fresha, Vagaro, or Booksy and hit walls on features that don't exist in those platforms — custom loyalty, VIP concierge workflows, franchise fee calculations.
  • You have a specific integration requirement — your POS, your CRM, your accounting system, your payroll — and need custom engineering to connect them.
  • You have a design or UX requirement that standard white-label can't hit — a fully bespoke mobile app with unique workflows, not just a reskin.

If you're not sure whether custom is the right call, start with the discovery call — we will tell you honestly whether Sicus Booking or White-Label is a better fit for your situation.

How we work

Fixed-scope proposals, weekly demos, no 3-month black box.

1

Free Discovery Call (30 min)

Tell us about your salon, your goals, and the problem you're trying to solve. We ask questions, challenge assumptions, and give you an honest read on whether custom is the right approach or whether Sicus Booking or White-Label would serve you better. No pitch.

2

Requirements Workshop (paid, ~1-2 weeks)

If the discovery call shows a fit, we run a paid requirements workshop where we dig deep: user flows, data models, integrations, edge cases, and the questions you haven't thought to ask yet. Output is a detailed scope document and a fixed-price proposal. The workshop fee is credited toward the build if you move forward.

3

Fixed-Price Proposal

You get a written proposal with line-item breakdown, timeline, milestones, acceptance criteria, and the ownership terms. Fixed scope, fixed price, no surprise overages. If you want changes after the proposal is signed, we quote them separately and you decide whether to add them.

4

Build with Weekly Demos

We build in 1-week sprints and you see a live demo every Friday — not a progress report, an actual clickable product. You spot issues early, we adjust early, and the end result matches what you actually want. No "we'll show you in 3 months."

5

Launch & Handoff

We deploy to production, run a launch period with you monitoring alongside us, and hand off documentation. From there you can either take the code in-house (if you own it) or keep us on a monthly retainer for maintenance, bug fixes, and feature iteration. Retainer is optional — no commitment.
