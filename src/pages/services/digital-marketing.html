Why Salons Need a Specialized Marketing Team

Most marketing agencies treat salons like any other small business. They run the same Facebook ads, write the same blog posts, and check the same boxes — and most of it doesn't work, because they don't understand the industry.

Salons live and die on local discovery, visual content, reviews, and retention. A salon's growth engine is fundamentally different from a restaurant's, a contractor's, or a tech startup's. Marketing that ignores this difference wastes money.

SICUS Media exclusively serves salon owners. Every recommendation we make, every ad we run, every piece of content we write is built on what we've learned working with nail, hair, and beauty businesses across North America.
