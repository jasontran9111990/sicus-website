Why a branded app instead of just using ours?

Your clients don't want to book "on SICUS." They want to book "at Bloom Nail & Beauty" — your brand, not ours. A branded app feels premium, reinforces client loyalty, lives on the home screen of their phone alongside Instagram and Apple Pay, and sends push notifications under your name. For high-end salons and mid-size chains, this is the difference between feeling like a local shop using software and feeling like a real brand.

Building a custom salon app from scratch costs $50,000 to $200,000+ and takes 6-12 months with an outside agency. And once it's built, you're on the hook for updates, iOS compliance, Android fragmentation, payment integration, SMS deliverability, security patches, and App Store review rejections. Most salon owners don't want that project.

Our white-label service gives you a branded app in 2-4 weeks at a fraction of the cost. You bring your brand. We bring the platform — same code that powers Sicus Booking, same AI, same bilingual support. When we ship improvements, your app gets them automatically.

How it works — 2 to 4 weeks from kickoff to launch

No 6-month agency project, no surprises, no rewrites.

1

Free Discovery Call

30-minute call to understand your salon, your brand, your launch timeline, and whether you need iOS, Android, web, or all three. You get a clear scope, a fixed quote, and an answer on whether white-label is the right fit — or whether you need a full Custom Salon Software build instead.

2

Brand Asset Kickoff (Week 1)

You send us your logo files, brand colors (hex codes), font preferences, and any existing photography. We provision your custom domain, set up SSL, and configure the app shell in your brand. You also fill out your salon intake form (services, staff, hours, pricing, policies) same as any Sicus Booking customer.

3

Staging Review (Week 2)

We hand you a live staging version of your app with your brand fully applied. You click through, test the booking flow, review copy, and request adjustments to colors, layouts, or wording. Two rounds of revisions are included; more are available if needed.

4

App Store Submission (Week 3)

We submit your app to the Apple App Store and Google Play Store under your salon's developer account (or under SICUS's developer account if you don't want to set one up). This takes 3-7 business days for review. We handle rejection responses and resubmissions. You get daily status updates.

5

Launch & Ongoing Support (Week 4+)

App is live. We help you announce it to existing clients with a launch campaign template. From then on, you have direct Slack or email access to our team for questions, and we push feature updates to your app automatically. Monthly performance reports included — bookings, chat volume, AI savings.
